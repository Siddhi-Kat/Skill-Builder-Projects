# 🚀 QuickBite Express: Crisis Analysis & Recovery Strategy

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes a business crisis faced by a food-tech platform and provides data-driven insights and recovery strategies.
The goal was to understand how operational failures and customer sentiment impacted business performance and how targeted strategies can drive recovery.

---

## 🚨 Problem Statement
Refer to `problem_statement.pdf` for full details.

---

## 📊 Analysis Covered

The analysis and insights are structured as follows:

- The Crisis  
- Business Impact  
- Customer Behavior  
- What Went Wrong  
  - Delivery Breakdown  
  - Food Quality Issues  
- Customer Recovery Strategy  
- Restaurant Partnerships  
- Delivery Optimization

## 🧠 For Learners

If you're trying to build a similar project, focus on:

- Structuring the problem into phases (pre-crisis vs crisis)  
- Defining meaningful metrics (churn, SLA breach, revenue impact)  
- Creating customer and operational segments  
- Connecting insights to business strategies
